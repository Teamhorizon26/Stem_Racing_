Core Concepts

Horizon's philosophy and methodology

Horizon Engineering
Horizon is a STEM Racing team operating under a defined structure where each area — engineering, design, manufacturing, and management — fulfills a specific function within the same system. The development of the single-seater is not an isolated task but the result of a controlled process that integrates analysis, decision-making, and technical execution.
EngineeringDesignFabrication
Our Approach
The team focuses on designing and fabricating its own modular car through structured methodologies, utilising modelling, simulation, and validation tools to optimise performance. Each vehicle iteration responds to data obtained from tests and evaluations, enabling progressive and measurable improvement.
ModellingSimulationData-Driven
Continuous Evolution
Horizon does not base its operation on isolated results but on process consistency. Discipline in time management, precision in decision-making, and coherence in execution reduce errors and increase team efficiency at each development phase.
DisciplinePrecisionCoherence
The Horizon Concept
The motto "Advancing Towards the Horizon" reflects Horizon's philosophy of maintaining constant, measurable progress with a clear direction in every project phase. "Advancing" represents continuous action and progressive improvement, "Towards" indicates a defined course guiding the team's decisions, and "the Horizon" symbolises dynamic goals and higher standards that steer the team's structured evolution.
OptimisationEvolution
